About IP Filter Exchange

IP Filter Exchange is a powerful Chrome extension that helps you connect to proxy servers safely and securely. Our extension provides automatic timezone matching, WebRTC leak protection, and support for multiple proxy protocols.

We prioritize your privacy - all settings and credentials are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers. With zero data collection and no tracking, you can browse with complete peace of mind.

Whether you need to test geo-restricted content, maintain privacy, or switch between different proxy locations, IP Filter Exchange makes it simple and secure.
